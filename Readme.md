### **Points to Remember:**

- Each participant can redeem the credits once per account.
- The credits are valid for 90 days (Trial Period).
- Credits cannot be combined, regardless of team size.
- Credits are non-transferable and cannot be shared between accounts.
- If credits are exhausted **no extra credits will be provided**. Please use them carefully.
- Credits cannot be used for any other purposes/platform.
- Any unused credits at the end of the 90-day period will expire automatically.
- Participants are responsible for ensuring they use the credits within the trial period.
- Any misuse or violation of the terms regarding credits may lead to disqualification from further access or offers.
- Credits are not refundable under any circumstances.

### **After the Trial-Period:**

- As a precaution, please disable autopay.
- Please refrain from reaching out in the future regarding any billing requests. We encourage you to work promptly within the given timelines.

### **How to Redeem Your Google Cloud Credits**

Here’s how you can redeem your credits and begin utilizing Google Cloud services.Please follow the provided instructions carefully:

1.  **Access the Platform:** Visit the Google Cloud Platform: https://cloud.google.com/?hl=en.
2.  **Sign Up for Free:** Sign-up/Sign-in using the registered email address

1.  Click on the **“Get started for free”** Button

1.  A new tab will open for redeeming the credits.
2.  Select the country(**India**) and click on “**Agree & continue**”

1.  Click on “**Create new payments profile**”

1.  Click on "**Profile type**" and select “**Individual**”. Once the necessary details are filled click on “**Create**”

1.  Click on **“Add payment method”**

1.  Select the **UPI QR code in the Payment method** (Payment method is only for verification) and click on “**Start free**”.

**IMPORTANT NOTE: ONLY “UPI QR CODE” OPTION SHOULD BE SELECTED IN PAYMENT METHOD**

1.  In the “**UPI QR code**” option a bar code will appear. **Scan the bar code with your preferred UPI app** and set up the auto pay request \[In image: **GPay**\]

**Note: An amount of 2/- will be debited for initial set up.**

1.  Once auto pay setup is done, the page will refresh automatically. A dialogue box will appear after the page loads, click on “**Close**”

1.  Hurray!!! You have successfully redeemed your credits.

**Disable auto pay from your source account on safer side to avoid billings in future**

### **How to Cancel Billing from Website**

Once the Prototype phase is over; follow the below steps to cancel the billing from the website.

1.  Click on the **Three dots** near the profile Picture and select “**Billing Account Management(Free Trial)**”

1.  Click on the three dots under “**Actions**” column and click on “**Disable billing**” as shown below

1.  Click on the “**Disable billing**” on the pop-up box.
